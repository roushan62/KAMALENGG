About Kamal Engineering

15+ years of building foundations, erecting scaffolds, and powering Gujarat's industrial growth.

Who We Are

Surat's Most Trusted Scaffolding Partner

Founded in 2010 in Surat, Gujarat, Kamal Engineering has grown from a small scaffolding contractor to one of the region's most respected industrial service providers. We specialize in scaffolding services, material on rent, manpower supply, and comprehensive industrial support.

Our team of 200+ skilled professionals has successfully executed over 500 projects across petrochemical refineries, power plants, construction sites, and chemical industries throughout Gujarat and Western India.

We are committed to delivering the highest standards of safety, quality, and on-time performance. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification and adherence to IS:3696 scaffolding safety standards reflect our unwavering commitment to excellence.
